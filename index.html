About Me

I am a Designer / Developer for an apartment marketing agency called Resite Online. I have a Computer Science Bachelors degree from ECPI University. My passion is to create bold and captivating designs to help bring company’s brands to life and solve problems. With a love for Digital Art, Photography, and Coding I have learned many related skills to help me knockout any project thrown at me. Whether it’s User Research, Wireframing, Hi-Fidelity Mockups, T-shirt or Logo design or Coding different animations or full websites. I solve problems and enjoy making things look cool while doing it.

Tools of the trade:

HTML, CSS, JS, PHP, GIT, NPM, Adobe CC, Sketch, Figma
